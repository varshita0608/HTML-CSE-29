Ready Player One is a 2018 American science fiction film based on Ernest Cline's 2011 novel Ready Player One.
The film is set in 2045, where much of humanity uses the OASIS, a virtual reality simulation, to escape the real world.

A teenage orphan finds clues to a contest that promises ownership of the OASIS to the winner, and he and his allies try to complete it before an evil corporation can do so.
